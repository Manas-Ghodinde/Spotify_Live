01 — Executive Summary

The gap is obvious.
Nobody has filled it.

Spotify ends at the stream. The moment a fan buys a ticket, pays for merch, or queues outside a venue — Spotify gets nothing. Live is where artists make real money and where fans feel real loyalty.

The Problem
Spotify's revenue stops at streaming. Live music — a $31B+ industry — sits entirely outside their platform.
The Opportunity
Spotify has the data, the audience, and the artist relationships. It just needs the product to connect them live.
The Approach
Two partnership tracks — one for established artists, one for emerging talent — each with distinct goals.
The Payoff
Artists earn more. Fans get exclusive access. Spotify becomes indispensable — not just convenient.
02 — Strategic Vision

Not just streaming.
The full music relationship.

Right now, Spotify is where you discover music. You find an artist, stream their album, maybe save them to a playlist. Then you go to a completely different app to buy a ticket. Another to follow them. Another to buy merch.

Every one of those handoffs is a moment Spotify loses you. The goal of Spotify Live is to close those loops — so the platform that introduced you to the artist is also the platform that connects you when they play live.

Make Spotify the place where the fan-artist relationship lives, not just where it starts.

Global Accessibility
Live music for fans who can't attend in person — same energy, different access point.
Artist Empowerment
New revenue beyond streams. Spotify becomes a partner in the whole career, not just discovery.
Real Fan Engagement
Live interaction passive streaming can't replicate — Q&As, exclusive drops, behind-the-scenes.
Platform Lock-In
When concerts, merch, and exclusive content all live on Spotify — why would you leave?
03 — Partnership Strategy

One product.
Two very different goals.

Established artists and emerging talent need completely different things from a live platform. Treating them the same would serve neither well.

Track 01
Spotify Studio Sessions
  • Exclusive studio recording sessions with high-quality production
  • Professional audio engineering and Spotify-branded live sets
  • Intimate acoustic performances streamed to global fanbase
  • Behind-the-scenes content and artist storytelling
  • Fan Q&A integration during live sessions
  • Merchandise drops tied to live events
  • Multi-platform content distribution rights
Track 02
Spotify Spotlight Program
  • Quarterly discovery cycles for emerging talent
  • Mentorship from established artists in the Spotify network
  • Marketing and algorithmic promotion support
  • Performance analytics and audience insights
  • Access to Spotify's production infrastructure
  • Development programs for touring and live skills
  • Clear pathway to Studio Sessions as audience grows

The Spotlight Program isn't charity — it's Spotify's next artist pipeline. Every emerging artist developed here becomes a Studio Sessions candidate in 12–24 months. The platform funds its own future supply of exclusive content.

04 — User Experience

From first listen
to live connection.

Every step is something a fan already wants to do. Spotify just makes it easier and keeps it in one place.

01
Discovery
Personalised recommendations surface live sessions from artists already in your library. "They're going live tonight."
02
Engagement
Real-time comments, reactions, and Q&A. Not passive — participatory. The artist can actually see you in the room.
03
Connection
Exclusive post-show content, merch drops, and early ticket access for fans who attended the live stream.
04
Retention
Notifications for future sessions, saved recordings in your library. The loop closes. You come back.
05 — Long-Term Vision

Start focused.
Scale deliberately.

The roadmap is phased — prove the product works before adding complexity. Every phase earns the next one.

2026
Foundation
Launch core platform. 20 Studio Sessions with established artists. Spotlight pilot with 50 emerging artists.
2027
Expansion
Scale to global markets. Ticket and merch integration. Advanced interactive features. 500+ Spotlight artists.
2028
Innovation
AR/VR live experience pilots. AI-powered session discovery. Premium super-fan tier launched.
2029+
Platform
Spotify becomes the primary live music destination globally — not a supplement to attending, an alternative to it.
